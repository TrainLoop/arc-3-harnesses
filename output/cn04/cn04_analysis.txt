This is a **connector puzzle game**. Each sprite has special **color-8 pixels** (connectors). You win a level when every color-8 pixel on every sprite overlaps with a color-8 pixel from another sprite (turning them green/color-3). You can move (up/down/left/right), rotate (90° CW), and click to select sprites. 150 action limit.

Here's how to solve each level:

---

## Level 1 (2 sprites)
**Sprites:** `vmgdxvpgkh` at (4,4) and `dzfylahsaw` at (12,9)

`vmgdxvpgkh` is auto-selected (closest to origin). Its connectors are at its right edge; `dzfylahsaw`'s connectors are at its left edge. Slide them together:

- **Move right 3, down 6** (9 actions)

This aligns all 4 connectors at global positions (12,11) and (12,13).

---

## Level 2 (3 sprites)
**Sprites:** `lejuhuvrjg` at (3,3), `grpgefeksy` at (4,11), `glojtydbea` at (12,4)

`lejuhuvrjg` is auto-selected. `grpgefeksy` is the hub with 4 connectors — the other two each plug into it.

1. **Move lejuhuvrjg down 6** (6 actions) → to (3,9)
2. **Click glojtydbea** to select it (1 action)
3. **Move glojtydbea left 4, down 8** (12 actions) → to (8,12)

Connectors pair at (5,12), (4,14), (10,12), and (8,17). **Total: 19 actions.**

---

## Level 3 (3 sprites)
**Sprites:** `hjqjqsmhmf` at (5,5), `gqxqpkuwab` at (11,4), `nhppemmlcd` at (9,11)

`nhppemmlcd` is the hub with 4 connectors (top pair + bottom pair). The other two plug in, but `hjqjqsmhmf` needs rotation first to match the horizontal spacing.

`hjqjqsmhmf` is auto-selected:

1. **Rotate 90°** (1 action)
2. **Move right 4, down 7** (11 actions) → to (9,12)
3. **Click gqxqpkuwab** to select it (1 action)
4. **Move left 2, down 5** (7 actions) → to (9,9)

Connectors pair at (10,11), (12,11), (10,14), and (12,14). **Total: 20 actions.**

---

## Level 4 (3 rotated sprites)
**Sprites:** `udepuflbbb` at (2,4) rot 90°, `ygyvhkssnz` at (9,9) rot 270°, `zhhaofpbyw` at (10,3) rot 90°

`udepuflbbb` is auto-selected (stays put — it's the anchor). All three sprites form a triangle of connectors.

1. **Click ygyvhkssnz** to select it (1 action)
2. **Move left 4, up 5** (9 actions) → to (5,4)
3. **Click zhhaofpbyw** to select it (1 action)
4. **Move left 4, down 6** (10 actions) → to (6,9)

Connectors pair at (6,4), (6,10), and (11,9). **Total: 21 actions.**

---

## Level 5 (4 sprites, hidden colors)
**Sprites:** `supigciwwi` at (2,3) rot 90°, `oncjjftokv` at (5,15) rot 90°, `dnkiufrohe` at (11,6), `lxgvcnpsir` at (14,13)

This level hides non-selected sprites (all shown as solid color-4). Two pairs need to overlap:
- `dnkiufrohe` + `oncjjftokv` (both have horizontal connector spacing of 4)
- `supigciwwi` + `lxgvcnpsir` (both have connector spacing of 5, but `lxgvcnpsir` needs a 90° rotation)

`supigciwwi` is auto-selected (stays put):

1. **Click oncjjftokv** to select it (1 action)
2. **Move right 6, up 9** (15 actions) → to (11,6), overlapping `dnkiufrohe`
3. **Click lxgvcnpsir** to select it (1 action)
4. **Rotate 90°** (1 action)
5. **Move left 12, up 5** (17 actions) → to (2,8), connectors align with `supigciwwi`

Connectors pair at (11,6), (15,6), (2,8), and (7,8). **Total: 35 actions.**
