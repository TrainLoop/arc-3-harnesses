## Game Overview: ls20

This is a **grid-based puzzle game** on a 64x64 board where you move a 5x5 player character in steps of 5 pixels. The core mechanic is **matching a carried icon's shape, color, and rotation** to what each goal tile requires, then stepping onto the goal tile.

### Controls
- **ACTION1** = Up (y-1), **ACTION2** = Down (y+1), **ACTION3** = Left (x-1), **ACTION4** = Right (x+1)
- Each move advances 5 pixels

### Key Elements
| Tag | Role |
|-----|------|
| `ihdgageizm` (yellow 5x5) | **Wall** — blocks movement |
| `rjlbuycveu` (gray 5x5) | **Goal tile** — step on it with matching icon to clear |
| `kvynsvxbpi` (diagonal) | **Goal indicator** — shows required shape/color/rotation |
| `npxgalaybz` (green ring) | **Step refill** — resets step counter to max |
| `ttfwljgohq` / `mkjdaccuuf` | **Shape changer** — cycles shape +1 (6 shapes total, wraps) |
| `soyhouuebz` | **Color changer** — cycles color +1 through [12→9→14→8→...] |
| `rhsxkxzdjz` | **Rotation changer** — cycles rotation +90° [0→90→180→270→...] |
| `gbvqrjtaqo` (pushers) | **Conveyor walls** — push you in their direction suffix (_t=up, _b=down, _l=left, _r=right) |

### Lives & Steps
- **3 lives** per level attempt
- **42 steps** per life, decremented by `StepsDecrement` (1 or 2) per move
- If steps run out → lose a life, reset position & icon, restore map

### General Tips
1. **Grab step refills early** — they fully restore your 42 steps
2. **Count modifier hits carefully** — overshooting wastes precious steps
3. **Watch for pushers** — they can help skip wall sections or trap you
4. **Moving modifiers** require timing — arrive at their track when they're nearby
5. **Lives are precious** — 3 per level, running out = game over
