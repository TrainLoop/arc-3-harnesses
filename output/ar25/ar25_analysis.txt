## General Winning Principles

1. **Identify the target symmetry** — targets are always arranged with reflective symmetry matching the available mirrors.
2. **Position mirrors at the axis of symmetry** between target clusters.
3. **Place pieces so original pixels cover one cluster** — reflections automatically fill the symmetric counterparts.
4. **Use ACTION5 (cycle) or ACTION6 (click)** to switch between selecting pieces vs. mirrors.
5. **Use ACTION7 (undo)** if you make a wrong move — no step cost.
6. **In 2-mirror levels**, you only need to fill one quadrant; the cascade fills the other three.
